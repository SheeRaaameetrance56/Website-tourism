Trincomalee
Map

Trincomalee is port city situated in northwest coast of Srilanka. Sea view side, port harbour and beach is major tourists pots. Coral reeves and scuba diving also available and boat fishing is famous in this destination. The Koneswaram Temple stands on Swami Rock cliff, a popular vantage point for blue-whale watching. The holy complex contains ornate shrines and a massive statue of Shiva.

Udawalawa
Map

Udawalawa National park is on boundry of sabaragamuwa and uwa province. The park has Udawalawa reservoir and Walawe reivar. Safari is most popular at Udawalawa. Wild asian elephants, Srilankan panther, peacocks and other wild animals can see in the park.

Yala
Map

Yala national park is on southern province in Srilanka. Yala was designated as a wildlife sanctuary in 1900, and, along with Wilpattu was one of the first two national parks in Sri Lanka, having been designated in 1938. The park is best known for its variety of wild animals.

Horton plains
Map

The Horton plains is a national park in the central highlands of Sri Lanka. It comprises a gently undulation highland plateau at the southern end of the central mountains massif of Sri Lanka. Surrounded by hill forests and it has major bio diversity. Also the most famous worlds end is situated in horton plains.
